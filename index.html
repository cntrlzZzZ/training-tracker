Friday boxing template

Warm-up 8–10 mins (skip/march + shadow + shoulder prep + footwork), then pick one main option:

Option A — Conditioning Focus
8 rounds: 2:00 on / 1:00 off
R1–2: jab+cross+movement
R3–4: add hooks
R5–6: add slips/rolls after combos
R7–8: busy output, still clean
Option B — Power + Quality
10 rounds: 1:30 on / 1:00 off
Each round: 3–5 hard combos → full reset → repeat
If shoulders/elbows start feeling dodgy: ditch weighted shadow boxing, shorten rounds, prioritise snappy technique.
Steps Ramp (NEAT) Fat loss support

Steps ramp = gradually increasing daily steps so you don’t wreck your calves/feet or spike hunger.

  • Week 1: 5–6k/day
  • Week 2: 7–8k/day
  • Week 3: 9–10k/day
  • Week 4+: stay around 9–10k most days
Easy method: 2 × 10-minute walks daily (after meals is elite).
